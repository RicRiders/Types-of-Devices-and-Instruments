Types of Devices and Instruments

Plotter: They are used for all types of construction and MCAD applications, including technical drawings, orthophoto maps, and rendering.

Trending: Derives from the noun train, meaning “trend” in English. It's about knowing what you're talking about.

Social media: A broad term covering social networks; it refers to all networks and media that have emerged in recent years.

Challenge: Derives from “challenge,” referring to a challenge or task to accomplish.

Youtuber streamer: A person who makes live or recorded broadcasts on a specific platform or service.

Influencer: A person with credibility on a specific topic.

Diseñador gráfico: Professionally conceptualizes and captures in images the message intended for a specific audience.

Manga: Japanese comics and graphic novels that follow a sequential narrative.

Bluetooth: Wireless technology used for short-range data transmission between devices.

Código QR: Quick response code, an evolution of the barcode that displays contained information when scanned.

Código de barra: A set of numbers and parallel lines forming a universal code.

Nodos: In computing and telecommunications, a node is an intersection point where multiple elements meet.

Character A unit of information that corresponds approximately to a grapheme or similar symbol.
Desktop Wallpaper The image on which desktop icons are placed.
Bit Used to represent the contrast between two values: off and on, false and true, open and closed.
Byte Standard unit of information used in computing and telecommunications.
Kilobyte Unit of information storage whose symbol is KB, equivalent to 1024 bytes.
Gigabyte A frequently used standard unit in computing that indicates storage capacity.
Terabyte Digital data measurement unit used to evaluate a specific amount of data or storage capacity.
Programs Set of logical steps written in a programming language that allows us to perform a specific task.
Software The formal system of a computer system, which includes the necessary logical components for implementation.
Hardware Physical part of a computer or computer system.
ICT's Enable the compilation, processing, storage, and transmission of information like voice, data, text, video, and images.
Web Information distribution system based on hypermedia linked and accessible through the internet.
Computer Networks A set of equipment connected by cables, signals, waves, or other data transport methods to share information and resources.
Internet Allows free information exchange among users.
Code Ensure applications or programs operate to enable communication between user and computer.
Tags Codes used to "mark up" text on a webpage to instruct browsers on display methods.
HTML Rack Structure and display of a web page and its content.
Switch Connects or joins devices within a network.
Router Sends internet data to personal devices, like computers and phones.
RJ-45 Cable Connects a device to the internet or another network device.
Includes storage devices, power source, GPU, motherboard, CPU, and RAM.
Laptop Often designed to support software and files.
DVD Optical disc capable of storing large amounts of images, sounds, or data in encoded form.
LCD Projector A projector used for displaying video, images, or informational data.
